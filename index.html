Who Are Suspicious

Overview

The project is based on Mini Challenge 2 in the 2021 IEEE VAST challenge. It presents a fictional company called GAStech, where several employees disappear during a celebration of a newfound fortune. The objective of the challenge is to analyze the provided data (gps.csv, cc_data.csv, loyalty_data.csv, car-assignment.csv) and determine which GAStech employees are potentially involved in suspicious activities.
