Who Are Suspicious

Overview

The project is based on Mini Challenge 2 in the 2021 IEEE VAST challenge. It presents a fictional company called GAStech, where several employees disappear during a celebration of a newfound fortune. The objective of the challenge is to analyze the provided data (gps.csv, cc_data.csv, loyalty_data.csv, car-assignment.csv) and determine which GAStech employees are potentially involved in suspicious activities.

Task 1 Popular Locations and Peak Times Using Card

The Bar Chart displays the frequency of the transactions of credit card and loyalty card at a location.

Insight: The most popular Location is Katerinas Cafe.

The Gantt Chart displays the timing and locations of credit card transactions, with the x-axis representing a 24-hour period and the y-axis representing different locations. This visualization provides insights into the peak transaction times and specific locations.

Insight: Certain cafes experience peak sales around noon. Additionally, there is a significant surge in popularity within the food and beverage category, particularly during non-working hours.

The box plot displays the price attribute of each transaction record for different locations.

One outlier with an unusually high value is observed in the chart. This outlier occurred at Frydos Autosupply n’ More Shop.

Task 3 Infer the Owners of Each Credit Card and Loyalty Card

Credit Card and Loyalty Card Mapping

The Sankey Diagram displays the matching of Credit Cards and Loyalty Cards based on timestamp, price, and location.

Insight: It is observed that one Credit Card can be matched to several Loyalty Cards, or one Loyalty Card can be matched to several Credit Cards.

The tree graph displays the owner of credit card and loyalty card owner
